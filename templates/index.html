Understanding the Intelligence

How accurate are the predictions?

Our Random Forest model is trained on 36,000+ data points from the official 2023-24 admission cycles. While year-on-year competition changes, our probability scoring provides an industry-leading approximation of your chances.

Why is Percentile more important than Rank?

Ranks fluctuate wildly based on the number of applicants. Percentile represents your standing relative to other test-takers, which remains the single most important factor for admission logic.

Can I see All-Maharashtra results?

Yes. By selecting "All Regions" in our Search Suite, you can see every college across the state that matches your percentile and category profile.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. EduPredict AI processes your percentile locally on our analysis engine and does not store your identifying information on its public servers.
